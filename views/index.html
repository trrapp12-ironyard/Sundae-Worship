Sundae Worship began like every good love story -- with two young people who are brought together over a scoop of ice-cream, the first blushes of love and copious amounts of unhealthy obsession. Except there was only a single, lonely person, an empty carton of Ben and Jerry's, but still with copious amounts of unhealthy obsession. This project began as an introductory project in a coding bootcamp. It was supposed to be a single static webpage which used HTML <table> tags to create a menu. Over the years I have come back to it again and again. The tables got dropped as I tried to experiment using JavaScript to import data from a file, then write the table programmatically. I was successful at that. But then that wasn’t enough. I thought why not just build a backend with MVC architecture. And I was successful at that. So here it is today. A beautiful project made with a little bit of love and an unwarranted amount of obsession.

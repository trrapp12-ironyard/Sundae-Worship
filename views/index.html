Sundae Worship began like every good love story -- with two young people who are brought together over a scoop of ice-cream, the first blushes of love and copious amounts of unhealthy obsession.

Except there was only one person (a lonely bachelor), no love, and only an empty carton of Ben and Jerry's to go with the copious amounts of unhealthy obsession.

This project began as an introductory project in a coding bootcamp. It was supposed to be a single static webpage which used HTML <table> tags to create a menu on a static website. Over the years I have come back to it again and again. The static tables made in html were updated to tables drawn with JavaScript.

I was successful at that.

But then that wasn’t enough. I thought why not just build a backend with MVC architecture. And I was successful at that. So here it is today. A beautiful project made with a little bit of love and an unwarranted amount of obsession.
